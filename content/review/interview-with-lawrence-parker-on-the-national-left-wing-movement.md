An interview with Lawrence Parker, the author of *[Communists and Labour: The National Left-Wing Movement, 1925-1929](http://www.lulu.com/shop/lawrence-parker/communists-and-labour-the-national-left-wing-movement-19251929/paperback/product-23727879.html)*, conducted by Efraim Carlebach of the Platypus Affiliated Society on July 21, 2018.

An [edited transcript of the interview](https://platypus1917.org/2018/11/02/those-twenties-an-interview-with-lawrence-parker-on-the-national-left-wing-movement/) was published in the Platypus Review Issue #111.
