On February 23th, 2010, Platypus hosted an event entitled Which Way Forward for Palestinian Liberation? in which [Joel Kovel](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/joel-kovel-event-speakers/), author of Overcoming Zionism and frequent commentator on the Israel-Palestine conflict, and [Hussein Ibish](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/hussein-ibish-event-speakers/), political analyst and senior fellow at The American Task Force on Palestine, answered questions posed by [Richard Rubin](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/richard-rubin-event-speakers/) of Platypus.

[Hussein Ibish](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/hussein-ibish-event-speakers/) is a senior fellow at the American Task Force on Palestine and is the executive director of the Hala Salaam Maksoud Foundation for Arab- American Leadership. From 1998-2004, he was Communications Director for the American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee (ADC), the largest Arab-American membership organization in the United States, with which he authored, along with Ali Abunimah, the issue-paper The Palestinian Right of Return. Ibish will discuss his recent book, What’s Wrong with the One State Agenda? Why Ending the Occupation and Peace with Israel is Still the Palestinian National Goal.

[Joel Kovel](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/joel-kovel-event-speakers/) is professor emeritus of social studies at Bard College, editor in chief of Capitalism, Nature, Socialism and the author of several books, including White Racism: A Psychohistory, A Complete Guide to Therapy, The Age of Desire: Case Histories of a Radical Psychoanalyst, The Radical Spirit, and The Enemy of Nature: The End of Capitalism or The End of the World. In 1998, he was the Green Party candidate for U.S. Senator from New York. He will be discussing his most recent book, Overcoming Zionism: Creating a Single Democratic State in Israel/Palestine.
