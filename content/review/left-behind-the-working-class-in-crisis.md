The Platypus Affiliated Society presents a moderated panel discussion and audience Q&A addressing issues of global capital, trade unions, workers rights, international solidarity, and immigration, in light of recent economic and political change. Held on Thursday April 23, 2009, at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

Speakers:

[Abraham Mwaura](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/abraham-mwaura-event-speakers/), United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America, organizer at the Republic Windows & Doors Factory.  
[Chuck Hendricks](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/chuck-hendricks-event-speakers/), Unite Here organizer  
[Aaron Hughes](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/aaron-hughes-event-speakers/), representative at the International Labor Conference Erbil Iraq, and member of Iraq Veterans Against the War  
[James Thindwa](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/james-thindwa-event-speakers/), executive director of Chicago Jobs with Justice
