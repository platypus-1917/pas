On Thursday March 11, 2010, Platypus Review Editor-in-Chief [Spencer A. Leonard](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/spencer-a-leonard-event-speakers/) interviewed the prominent 1960s radical and last National Secretary of Students for a Democratic Society (SDS), [Mark Rudd](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/mark-rudd-event-speakers/), to discuss his recently published political memoir, Underground. In April, Leonard’s interview with Rudd, prepared in conjunction with [Atiya Khan](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/atiya-khan-event-speakers/), was broadcast in two parts on “Radical Minds” on WHPK-FM 88.5 Chicago.
