The *Platypus Review* is a *free* monthly broadsheet produced and distributed that acts as an open forum for discussions about the left ([full statement of purpose](http://platypus1917.org/2007/11/01/platypus-review-editorial-statement-of-purpose/)).

The *Platypus Review* is distributed in and around campus at the following locations:

1. Ohio Union (Ohio State University, [map](http://goo.gl/maps/RsBgW))  
2. Kafe Kerouac (2250 High St., [map](http://goo.gl/maps/lEAyF))

3. Sporeprint Infoshop (172 E 5th Avenue, [map](http://goo.gl/maps/O48Pe))

4. Capital University's Blackmore Library (1 College Avenue, [map](http://goo.gl/maps/SVLnm))
