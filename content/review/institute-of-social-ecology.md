# **Platypus PCVII Workshop**

## with Peter Staudenmaier of the Institute of Social Ecology

Moderator: David MountainOn Saturday, April 11, 2015, Platypus invited members from various groups on the Left to lead discussions on the politics of their project at the Seventh Annual Platypus International Convention. This is audio from the discussion led by Peter Staudenmaier of the Institute of Social Ecology.

Every year at the Platypus International Convention, speakers from various perspectives are asked to bring their experience of the Left’s recent history to bear on today’s political possibilities and challenges as part of the “Differing Perspectives on the Left” workshop series.
