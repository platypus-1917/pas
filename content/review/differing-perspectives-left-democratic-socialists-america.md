Every year at the Platypus International Convention, speakers from various perspectives are asked to bring their experience of the Left’s recent history to bear on today’s political possibilities and challenges as part of the “Differing Perspectives on the Left” workshop series.

A workshop with the Democratic Socialists of America ([Joseph Schwartz](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/joseph-schwartz/) (Temple University) and [Bill Barclay](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/bill-barclay/)) held on April 5, 2014, at the Sixth Platypus International Convention.
