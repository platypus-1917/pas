At the 1st annual international convention of the Platypus Affiliated Society, in Chicago, June 12-14, 2009, the concluding plenary event, a discussion on Platypus's theoretical stance, its raison d'etre, and where the project will be going.

[Richard Rubin](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/richard-rubin-event-speakers/) speaks on "Four Types of Ambiguity;" [Chris Cutrone](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/chris-cutrone-event-speakers/) speaks on "History, Theory;" and [Ian Morrison](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/ian-morrison-event-speakers/) speaks on "What is to be Done?" Audience question-and-answer discussion follows. Held on June 14th, 2009 at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.
