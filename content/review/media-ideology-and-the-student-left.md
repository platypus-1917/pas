The Platypus Affiliated Society hosted a panel discussion on the Politics of the Contemporary Student Left at the U.S. Social Forum (USSF) in Detroit on June 26, 2010. Moderated by [Laurie Rojas](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/laurie-rojas-event-speakers/), assistant editor for the Platypus Review, the panel consisted of [Will Klatt](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/will-klatt-event-speakers/), member of the new Students for a Democratic Society (SDS) and organizer for Service Employees International Union (SEIU); [Luis Brennan](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/luis-brennan-event-speakers/), a student organizer at University of Chicago and former member of the new SDS; [Aaron Petcov](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/aaron-petcov-event-speakers/), formerly of the new SDS and currently a member of the Organization for a Free Society (OFS); and [Ashley Weger](http://platypus1917.org/category/event-speakers/ashley-weger-event-speakers/), an organizer for Platypus and a former organizer for UNITE HERE.
