Every Wednesday, 5:30–7:30 pm  
New North 204, Georgetown University (37th and O St NW, Washington, DC 20057)

Everyone is welcome!

( • required / + recommended readings)

**Recommended background preliminary readings:**   
+ Benjamin Constant, ["The liberty of the ancients compared with that of the moderns"](http:/uploads/2014/10/constant_liberty.pdf) (1819)  
+ J. P. Nettl, [“The German Social Democratic Party 1890–1914 as a Political Model”](/uploads/readings/nettljp_spd.pdf) (1965)

**Recommended supplemental parallel readings:**   
+ Chris Cutrone, "[The end of the Gilded Age](https://platypus1917.org/2017/12/02/end-gilded-age-discontents-second-industrial-revolution-today/)" (2017) and "[Gilded Age socialism -- historically past?](https://chriscutrone.platypus1917.org/?p=3488)" (2023)   
+ Cutrone, "[Lenin's liberalism](https://platypus1917.org/2011/06/01/lenins-liberalism/)" and "[Lenin's politics](https://platypus1917.org/2011/09/25/lenins-politics/)" (2011)   
+ Cutrone, "[What is political party for Marxism?](https://platypus1917.org/2014/11/04/political-party-marxism/)" (2014)   
+ Cutrone, "[Back to Herbert Spencer! Industrial vs. militant society](https://platypus1917.org/2016/01/12/back-to-herbert-spencer/)" (2016) [[audio](https://youtu.be/g2s2oImyPqg)]  
+ Cutrone, "[Horkheimer in 1943 on party and class](https://platypus1917.org/2016/01/12/horkheimer-in-1943-on-party-and-class/)" (2016)   
+ Max Horkheimer, "[On the sociology of class relations](https://nonsite.org/max-horkheimer-and-the-sociology-of-class-relations/)" (1943)   
+ Cutrone, "[The dictatorship of the proletariat and the death of the Left](https://platypus1917.org/2021/11/01/the-dictatorship-of-the-proletariat-and-the-death-of-the-left/)" (2021)   
+ August Nimtz, Andrew Arato and Chris Cutrone, "[Socialism, liberalism and Marxism](https://youtu.be/eGVVoNmjPGo)" (January 6, 2021)
