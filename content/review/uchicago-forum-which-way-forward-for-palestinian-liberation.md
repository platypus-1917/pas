The Platypus Affiliated Society, The International House Global Voices Lecture Series, and UChicago Students for Justice in Palestine present

**Which Way Forward for Palestinian Liberation?**

**One state or two?  
A discussion with Hussein Ibish and Joel Kovel.**

**Tuesday, February 23** | 7PM

[International House](http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&client=firefox-a&ie=UTF8&q=international+house+uchicago&fb=1&gl=us&hq=international+house+uchicago&hnear=Chicago,+IL+60626&cid=0,0,10446413622759011675&ei=AIJ7S-_tMtHUngfR-dSvAw&ved=0CAoQnwIwAA&ll=41.790337,-87.590926&spn=0.006671,0.015986&z=16&iwloc=A) | 1414 E. 59th St.

[![](http://chicago.platypus1917.org/uploads/2010/02/Which-Way-Forward-for-Palestinian-Liberation-Flier-194x300.jpg "Which Way Forward for Palestinian Liberation - Flier")](http://chicago.platypus1917.org/uploads/2010/02/Which-Way-Forward-for-Palestinian-Liberation-Flier.jpg)

[Hussein Ibish](http://www.ibishblog.com/about_ibish "Hussein Ibish") is a senior fellow at the American Task Force on Palestine and is the executive director of the Hala Salaam Maksoud Foundation for Arab- American Leadership. From 1998-2004, he was Communications Director for the American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee (ADC), the largest Arab-American membership organization in the United States, with which he authored, along with Ali Abunimah, the issue-paper *[The Palestinian Right of Return](http://www.adc.org/issue_papers/ror/issuepaper30.PDF)*. Ibish will discuss his recent book, [*What's Wrong with the One State Agenda? Why Ending the Occupation and Peace with Israel is Still the Palestinian National Goal*](http://www.americantaskforce.org/in_media/pr/2009/08/28/1251432000).

[Joel Kovel](http://www.joelkovel.org/joelkovel.html "Joel Kovel") is professor emeritus of social studies at Bard College, editor in chief of *[Capitalism, Nature, Socialism](http://www.cnsjournal.org/)* and the author of several books, including *White Racism: A Psychohistory, A Complete Guide to Therapy,* *The Age of Desire: Case Histories of a Radical Psychoanalyst, The Radical Spirit,* and *The Enemy of Nature: The End of Capitalism or The End of the World*. In 1998, he was the Green Party candidate for U.S. Senator from New York. He will be discussing his most recent book, [*Overcoming*](http://goog_1266365499884/) *[Zionism: Creating a Single Democratic State in Israel/Palestine](http://www.plutobooks.com/display.asp?K=9780745325699&).*

Hosted by the University of Chicago Platypus Affiliated Society. Cosponsored by [UChicago Students for Justice in Palestine](https://lists.uchicago.edu/web/info/sjpalestine), [the International House Global Voices Lecture Series](http://ihouse.uchicago.edu/programs/programs_gv_lectures.shtml), and the University of Chicago Student Government.

Free and Open to the Public.

RSVP online at the [Facebook event](http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=474569935639).
