Platypus London: Goldsmiths College: Intro Reading Group, Autumn 2014

Mondays 7pm, Room TBC – check facebook for details: https://www.facebook.com/groups/londonplatypus

Week 1 (29 Sep 2014)

- Cutrone, [“Symptomology: Historical transformations in social-political context”](http://platypus1917.org/2009/05/15/symptomology/)
- Cutrone, [“Capital in history: The need for a Marxian philosophy of history of the Left”](http://platypus1917.org/2008/10/01/capital-in-history-the-need-for-a-marxian-philosophy-of-history-of-the-left/)

Week 2 (6 Oct 2014)

- Kolakowski, [“The concept of the Left”](http:/uploads/readings/kolakowskileszek_conceptleft1968.pdf)
- Adorno, [“Imaginative excesses”](http:/uploads/readings/adorno_imaginativeexcesses.pdf)

Week 3 (13 Oct 2014)

- Blumberg, Cutrone, Khan, Leonard, and Rubin, [Forum: The decline of the Left in the 20th century](http://platypus1917.org/the-decline-of-the-left-in-the-20th-century/)

Week 4 (20 Oct 2014)

- Anderson, Cutrone, Kreitman, Postel, and Turl, [Forum: Imperialism: What is it, why should we be against it?](http://platypus1917.org/2010/07/09/imperialism-what-is-it-why-should-we-be-against-it/)
- Albert, Cutrone, Duncombe, and Holmes, [Forum: The 3 Rs: reform, revolution and “resistance:” The problematic forms of “anti-capitalism” today](http://platypus1917.org/2008/04/01/the-3-rs-reform-revolution-and-resistance-the-problematic-forms-of-anticapitalism-today-2/)

Week 5 (27 Oct 2014)

- Brennan, Davis, Hendricks, Mujica, and Rubin, [Forum: What is a movement?](http://platypus1917.org/2009/08/24/what-is-a-movement-pr/)
- Hendricks, Hughes, Mwaura, and Thindwa, [Forum: Left behind: The working class in the crisis](http://platypus1917.org/2009/07/01/left-behind-the-working-class-in-the-crisis/)

*[3 Nov 2014 – No meeting – Goldsmiths’ reading week*]

Week 6 (10 Nov 2014)

- Platypus Historians Group, [Catastrophe, historical memory, and the Left: 60 years of Israel-Palestine](http://platypus1917.org/2008/05/01/catastrophe-historical-memory-and-the-left-60-years-of-israel-palestine/)
- Ibish, Kovel, and Rubin, [Forum: Which way forward for Palestinian liberation?](http://platypus1917.org/2010/04/08/which-way-forward-for-palestinian-liberation-2/)
- Goodman and Rubin, [Forum: Marxism and Israel](http://platypus1917.org/2011/05/05/marxism-and-israel-left-perspectives-on-the-israeli-palestinian-conflict/)

Week 7 (17 Nov 2014)

- Farrow, Gabrellas, Mucciaroni, and Wolf, [Forum: Which way forward for sexual liberation?](http://platypus1917.org/2011/02/01/which-way-forward-for-sexual-liberation/)
- Nogales, Pereira Di Salvo, and Rojas, [Forum: Politics of the contemporary student Left](http://platypus1917.org/2009/09/30/politics-of-the-contemporary-student-left/)
- Brennan, Klatt, Petcov, and Weger, [Forum: Ideology and the student Left](http://platypus1917.org/2010/09/12/ideology-and-the-student-left/)

Week 8 (24 Nov 2014)

- Bernstein, Cutrone, Goehr, and Horowitz, [Forum: The relevance of Critical Theory to art today](http://platypus1917.org/2011/01/01/the-relevance-of-critical-theory-to-art-today/)
- Cutrone, Feenberg, Westerman, and Brown, [Platypus convention plenary: The politics of Critical Theory](http://platypus1917.org/2011/07/09/the-politics-of-critical-theory/)

Week 9 (1 Dec 2014)

- Horkheimer and Adorno, [“Discussion about Theory and Praxis” (AKA “Towards a New Manifesto?”)](http:/uploads/2011/12/horkheimeradorno_newmanifesto_NLR65_2010press.pdf)[[Deutsch](http:/uploads/2011/12/horkheimeradorno_theorieundpraxis1956.pdf)] (1956)
- Horkheimer, [selections from Dämmerung](http:/uploads/readings/horkheimer_dawnex.pdf)
- Adorno, [“Resignation”](http://home.comcast.net/~platypus1919/adorno_resignation1969.pdf)
- Cutrone, [“The Marxist hypothesis”](http://platypus1917.org/2010/11/06/the-marxist-hypothesis-a-response-to-alain-badous-communist-hypothesis/)
- Cutrone, [“The Left is dead! — Long live the Left!” Vicissitudes of historical consciousness and the possibilities for emancipatory social politics today](http://platypus1917.org/2007/11/01/vicissitudes-of-historical-consciousness-and-possibilities-for-emancipatory-social-politics-today/)

Week 10 (8 Dec 2014)

- Cutrone, Morrison, and Rubin, [Platypus convention plenary: The Platypus synthesis: History, theory, and practice](http://platypus1917.org/the-platypus-synthesis/)
