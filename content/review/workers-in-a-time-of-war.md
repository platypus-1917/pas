# Workers in a time of War:

## Pakistan and the Crisis of the Labour Movement

[Sunday, December 2, 2:00 PM](http://chicago.platypus1917.org/calendar/) |  [International House](http://ihouse.uchicago.edu/programs/programs_gv.shtml#LS)  
([1414 E. 59th Street, Chicago IL](http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1414+E.+59th+St.&oe=utf-8&rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&client=firefox-a&um=1&ie=UTF-8&hq=&hnear=1414+E+59th+St,+Chicago,+IL+60637&gl=us&ei=e0cMS_fxEqagM8_n1fMM&sa=X&oi=geocode_result&ct=title&resnum=1&ved=0CAgQ8gEwAA))

#### 

**A Discussion with**

**Gulzar Ahmed Chaudhary**General Secretary, [All Pakistan Trade Union Federation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/All_Pakistan_Trade_Union_Federation)

**Rubina Jamil**President, Working Women Organization

**Yasir Gulzar**President, [Progressive Youth Organization](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Progressive_Youth_Organization)

**[Atiya Khan](http://platypus1917.org/tag/atiya-khan/)**Platypus, Phd candidate at the University of Chicago

![A demonstration in Lahore, 2008, led by the Labour Party Pakistan.](http://chicago.platypus1917.org/uploads/2009/11/SITE-PAKISTAN-WOMEN.jpg "SITE-PAKISTAN-WOMEN")

A demonstration in Lahore, 2008, led by the Labour Party Pakistan.

A moderated panel discussion and audience Q&A bringing together three leading figures of the Pakistani labor movement to talk about workers rights, women’s rights, the struggle to organize in the shadow of the Taliban, and the impact of the ongoing war in Afghanistan on the workers of Pakistan.  These topics will be explored in light of the increasingly pressing need to reconstitute an international Left.

For background reading, see [The Failure of Pakistan: A Concise History of the Left]( http://platypus1917.org/2008/02/01/the-failure-of-pakistan-a-concise-history-of-the-left/).
